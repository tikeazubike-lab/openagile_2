## Unit Trust Schemes

### Abacus Unit Trust (#83)
- **Type:** Unit Trust Scheme / Mutual Fund
- **Note:** This is a fund, not a common stock
- **Status:** Active (if still operating)
- **Action:** Contact the fund administrator directly for:
  - Current unit value
  - Redemption process
  - Statement of holdings
  - Fund performance reports

## Important Notes

1. **Unit trusts are managed differently** from common stocks - they are priced once daily (NAV) rather than traded on an exchange
2. **Registrars** manage shares but are not investments themselves
3. If you believe you have investments through these entities, contact them directly for your account information
